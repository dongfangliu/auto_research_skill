## Raw Idea

Define recovery quality as a first-class metric for interrupted research tasks.

## Why It Matters

Without a recovery metric, the system cannot tell whether structured cards are better than a normal summary.

## Possible Contribution

A repeatable evaluation protocol for research-agent continuity.

## Counterarguments

- Recovery quality may be too context-dependent.
- Metrics might reward shallow checklist completion rather than good research judgment.

## Literature Questions

- How do task-resumption studies measure context recovery?
- Are there existing provenance completeness metrics?

## Minimum Verification Path

Define a simple rubric: active state recovered, next action recovered, evidence limits preserved, no unsupported claim introduced.

## Gate Decision

Proceed to hypothesis design after one real literature pass.
