## Evidence Statement

What was observed or established?

## Source

Experiment, literature note, dataset, figure, command, or artifact.

## Supports

Ideas, hypotheses, or claims supported by this evidence.

## Conflicts With

Ideas, hypotheses, or claims weakened by this evidence.

## Strength Assessment

Why the evidence level is weak, moderate, strong, or contradicted.

## Limitations

Scope, assumptions, uncertainty, and failure modes.

## Reuse Guidance

How this evidence may be used in claim or manuscript drafting.
