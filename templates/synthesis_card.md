## Consumption Notes

- `continue`: use this card to recover recent experiment-thread understanding.
- `experiment`: use the branch verdict to choose continue, revise, branch, park, or stop.
- `evidence`: review evidence candidates through Evidence Promotion before claim use.
- `claim`: use claim warnings and mainline impact only through Claim Audit.
- `manuscript`: use this card for route context, not publication wording.
